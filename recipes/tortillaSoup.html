Chicken Tortilla Soup

Description

This chicken tortilla soup recipe with shredded chicken, black beans and corn is easy to prepare, filling, and will take you to flavor town. Add some chopped fresh avocado, Monterey Jack cheese, crushed tortilla chips, or green onions to enhance the flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 cup whole corn kernels, cooked
  • 1 cup white hominy
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • crushed tortilla chips
  • sliced avocado
  • shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • chopped green onions

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat the onion, garlic, chili powder, oregano, and tomatoes over medium heat until the onions are translucent.
  2. Add the chicken broth, water, corn, and hominy. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer until the corn is tender, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the chile peppers, beans, cilantro, and chicken. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the tortilla chips and avocado. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the Monterey Jack cheese and green onions. Stir to combine.
  6. Serve with lime wedges.
