Steve's Chicken Noodle Soup

Description

This chicken noodle soup will blow your breath away. It is easily made with a wonderful flavorful chicken noodle soup that will cure any common disease and make your guests wanting to come back.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 6 stalks celery, diced
  • 6 carrot, diced
  • 3/4 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 3/4 tablespoon chopped fresh tarragon
  • 3/4 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 3/4 tablespoon chopped Italian flat leaf parsley
  • 4 quarts low-fat, low sodium chicken broth
  • 3 1/2 cups cubed skinless, boneless chicken breast meat
  • 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onions and celery and cook until the onions are translucent. Add the carrots and cook until the carrots are translucent. Add the rosemary, tarragon, thyme, and parsley. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  2. Add the chicken broth and cook until the broth is slightly thickened.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Add the noodles and cook until the noodles are tender.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve with a side of rice.
